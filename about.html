About Invictus


Invictus 2019 is the second edition of the Unified Techfest, being organized jointly by all the technical societies of Delhi Technological University. It will be a 3 day long technical fest from 8-10 February 2019.

About the events


Carrying on the legacy of the technical fests at Delhi Technological University, this year's fest will again be a platform for budding technocrats to explore and put their talent to test when they get to compete with other prodigies of their field in the nation. The techfest will cover a wide variety of events mainly in the technical domain with some other miscellaneous events. The events range from as conventional as Coding and Hardware events to as advanced as Aerial Robotics and Machine Learning. The whole fest will be divided into zones where you would find same genre events and exciting cash prizes would await. The fest would cater to all kind of technocrats with skills ranging from soft skills to coding skills, hardware prowess to simulation and designing wizards. With a footfall of 15k+ people from all over India, Invictus has arguably become one of the largest Techfest of North India.
